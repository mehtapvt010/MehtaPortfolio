Characters: One of The Best Aspects of The Show

Bobby: Everyone who knows Bobby, Loves Bobby

A veteran hunter, an old friend of the Winchesters' father and over time evolved into a father-figure for his sons Sam and Dean. He is also one of the longest lasting recurring characters, apart from Sam and Dean. Bobby equals badass and bobby equals cute, and if someone can be badass savage and cute at the same time, just like Dean, what more do you want from a character.

Castiel: Intense and then Cool and then Intense Again

Castiel is a strong, determined, impulsive, competitive and naïve celestial being. Like most angels, Castiel expresses little to no emotion, which creates flaws and complexities in his persona. Castiel was absolutely confident in the justice of Heaven. Now he’s not sure anymore, and that makes him unpredictable. What is predictable is his lack of normal social skills. He doesn’t respect personal space, doesn’t understand jokes, and is confused by Dean’s constant use of colloquial language.

Crowley: The Perfect Example of Character Development

A Personal Favorite, Crowley is probably the only demon(and Interim Devil) that I want to be friends with. Potrayed as a cunning and heartless demon when the character was introduced, Crowley went on to become a Lonely, longing for love and friendship, kind of a guy, who was more than what people would expect from someone of his designation. He is funny, he is a badass and he can be needy sometimes, but never monotonous.

Jack: He is wierd and overly kind, guess he is alright.

Jack Kline (b. May 18, 2017) (doesn't look like a 5 year old, does he?) is the son of Lucifer and Kelly Kline. He is the first and only known Nephilim to be sired by an Archangel. Jack was taken in by Sam Winchester and Dean Winchester who would teach him how to master his powers. During this time, Jack played a role in the resurrection of Castiel who he had chosen as his father.
