The Journey of Two Brothers who Conquered Angels, Demons and even God.

Carry on my Wayward Son; There'll be peace when you are done.
Put your weary head to rest; Don't you cry no more.

It will make you laugh, it will make you cry. You can watch it for the supernatural or you can watch it for the characters. You can watch it for the story or you can watch it for the brotherhood. Supernatural takes its viewer on a long journey, which will be full of emotions, surprises, joy, sorrow and connection. Every episode ends on a different note, but one message that is constant throughout the show is that No Matter What, Family Always Comes First.
If you have seen the show, you already know what it has to offer, and for those who haven't yet, you really need to.
