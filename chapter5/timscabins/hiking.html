The Cabins on the Beach

Go to the Beach

Uncle Tim's Cabins has a path right to the National Seashore, or pack the car and go to many of the beaches in the area.

Hiking

Uncle Tim's Cabins has 3 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to the National Seashore. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Bike Trail

The Bike trail right off the back of the camp is available for walking, roller blading or just taking a nice stroll.
