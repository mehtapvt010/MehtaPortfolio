The Cabins on the Beach

Our Cabins
Our luxury Cabins are permanent structures 2 feet off the ground. Each cabin has cedar walls, a wooden floor, and a skylight that can be opened.
How are the cabins furnished?
Each cabin is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring a bathing suit and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
