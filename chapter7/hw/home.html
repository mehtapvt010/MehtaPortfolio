This is just a fan page created by Shivang Mehta, to appreciate the awesome band, Queen.

The Members included:

  • Freddie Mercury: Vocals

  • Brian May: Guitar

  • Roger Taylor: Drums

  • John Deacon: Bass

This is a link to their Official website.

Some of their best albums are:

A Night at the Opera
This one is considered by many, one of the best albums in rock history and its marquee track, The Bohemian Rhapsody, is a bone-chilling and cool masterpiece, that one always ends up singing along.
Innuendo
This album was Queen's 14th and their last before Freddie's death. Tracks like Innuendo, The Show Must Go On, etc. were te highlights of this wonderful album.
News of the World
There's so much I can say about this one, but I will limit it to "We will, we will, rock you!!!". Other songs of this album were cool as well, and all in all, it is one of the most iconic rock albums ever.
